2011

android music-sheet app ↓

I joined a friends' team to participate Alten ID'Mobile contest first edition.Our project was to create a musicsheet app that would manage your collection and also help youwhile you're playing : automatically flip pages, point out the notes you misplayed ..This was maybe overly ambitious and we had a hard time figuring out how to correctly handle the FFTs,time-windows and mic-sampling problems. We managed to build a prototype that could recognize a simplepredefined tune (played by a computer), and flip the pages accordingly.We had also started work on generalizing it to live instruments, and to any music sheet (using lilypond),but it was not finished on time.
